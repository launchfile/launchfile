Construction

The logo uses layered SVG construction with no global clip-path:

  1. Background — Gradient-filled rounded rect (rx=22%)
  2. Glow — Radial gradient in purple-400
  3. Grid — Individual lines that bleed past rounded corners
  4. Arrow — Behind the glass, emerging from the frosted pocket
  5. Frosted glass — Lower half, clipped to rounded rect
  6. Border — Hairline stroke on the rounded rect

Grid lines extend past corners (unclipped). Glass is clipped. Shadow is external (CSS, not in SVG).
